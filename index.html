About

Where systems meet soul.

I'm a Software Systems student who designs with the precision of an engineer and the eye of an artist. Every interface I build starts with one question: what does the person actually need?

Problem-solving is my instinct, whether it's untangling a complex user flow or debugging a broken layout. I approach design as a system: logical, purposeful, and interconnected.
